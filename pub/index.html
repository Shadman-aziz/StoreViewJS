StoreViewJS

This library allows you to make store front for any marketplace

Home API 
   //make collection of cards 
  const collection = new CardCollection()
  collection.makeCollection('.FirstOne',"False","False","True")

you can add new items and they will be perfectly aligned in a grid format

(check documentation for what parameters you need to provide to make your very own card)

   //add card to collection 
    collection.makeCard('Spain','images/spain_pics/',3,'Spain Trip Package',
    'Welcome to the holiday of a lifetime',40,2500,'incredible scenes and food in Spain,
    treat yourself to an incredible atmosphere for Soccer and parties, this is one trip never to Forget')

you can enable "click to enlarge" functionality as well

These cards are enlargeable, click their Image to enlarge them (if you hover over the card long enough you can see animation of the different angles of the product as well) 

 
    //make collection of cards that can be enlarged 
      const collection2 = new CardCollection()
      collection2.makeCollection('.SecondOne',"False","True")

you can enable "add To cart" functionality as well

If you hover over these cards you will see the cart option pop up

Later I will show you the option to go to cart

 
    //make collection of cards that have "add to cart" feature 
        const collection3 = new CardCollection()
        collection3.makeCollection('.ThirdOne',"True","True")

you can also sort the items by price, in ascending order or descending order

Click the buttons and see for yourself

 
    //how to sort by price 
        collection4.sortByPrice()
        // or 
        collection4.sortByPrice('descending')

you can also switch the ordering of the cards

press the button below to switch the first and third card:

 
    //how to switch cards 
        collection5.switchPosition(0,2)

you can also only view the ones the customer liked, press the like button of the items you like

and then press the 'View Liked' button:

 
      //how to show liked cards 
          collection7.showLiked()

Once you have created an item card, you can still edit the content

enter the new title, new text and new price of any of the above cards(number starts at 0) (please fill in all of them and do not leave them blank)

 
     //how to edit a particular card 
    collection6.editTitle(document.getElementById('cardNum').value,document.getElementById('newTitle').value)
    collection6.editPrice(document.getElementById('cardNum').value,document.getElementById('newPrice').value)
    collection6.editContent(document.getElementById('cardNum').value,document.getElementById('newText').value)

Once you have created an item card, you can highlight it to get the customers attention (ex: ongoing sale/promotion)

you can highlight multiple items at a time,a maybe promote a certain sale

 
   //how to highlight card 
      collection10.highlightCard(0)

   //how to remove highlight from card 
      collection10.removeHighlightCard(0)

Click any of these items' cart option

If you hover over these cards you will see the cart option pop up

and then click the 'go to cart' button

the submit button of the cart will print out a JSON object with the buyer information in the console for the developer to use

 
    //how to show cart 
        collection8.checkoutCart()

Click any of these items' wishList option (the heart symbol)

and then click the 'show wishList' button

the exit button of the wishlist will repopulate the previous card grid

 
    //how to show wishlist 
        collection9.showWishList()

You can also delete any card from the grid (example: when an item is out of stock) 

 
      //how to delete particular card 
          collection11.deleteCard(0)
